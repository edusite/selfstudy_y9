our History

A group of Year-9 Indigo students from EDUSN International School who are enthusiastic about exhibiting what they have learned throughout the school year came up with the idea of creating a website that can assist lower secondary students, upcoming year-9 ones in particular, with their studies in Science. We supposed that the short notes in each unit rather than lengthy paragraphs as in coursebooks could help students understand the lessons better. Our class notes make up 85% of the content along with the help of further research in order to provide a good source for learners. In addition, we have also included a couple of tools the learners can use to create a better and more fun learning environment for them.
